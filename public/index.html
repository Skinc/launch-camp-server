High Schoolers: This summer don't get a job.

Make a job.

Found a profitable business in two weeks with Launch Camp.

We believe every young person should found a business.

Last year, we turned an Olin College of Engineering course into a two week intersession at Design Tech High School.

The class, Two Week Business, helps thirty 9th and 10th graders turn their passion into profit each year.

Launch Camp expands the course into an 80 hour intensive experience, designed to help students achieve more ambitious results.

We provide:

World class coaches

Top of the line equipment

Grit

Students' ideas won't work at first, but by the end of the two weeks they'll have one which is profitable.

Teamwork

Students ideate, prototype, market and sell all as a team.

Resourcefulness

Students will discover how to use their resources - including their own passions - to create value.

A session lasts two weeks, the network lasts forever.

We support Launch Camp graduates as they grow their businesses, apply for college and stay connected with friends from their cohort.
