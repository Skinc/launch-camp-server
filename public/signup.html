Launch Camp is $999 for each two week session. Students keep their profit. We enroll entering 9th-12th graders.

Camp dropoff opens at 8:30 AM and pickup is at 3:30 PM. We offer extended hours in the form of "Crunch Time" from 3:30 PM to 5:30 PM. Crunch Time costs an additional $30 per day or $200 per session. You can register for Crunch Time at the start of a session, or on the day you need it.

While there may be some food available for purchase from student businesses, students must bring their own lunch.

We'll reach out to confirm your registration and collect personal health info, emergency contacts and releases within a few days. If you'd like to be in touch sooner please email us at info@thelaunchcamp.com or call 240-603-2654.
